Incremental Search

Given f(x) = 0, we apply the following steps:

  1. The continuity of f must be guaranteed with theoretical arguments.
  2. We choose a starting value x0 and a Δx that expresses the size of the interval that we wish to find.
  3. We generate a sequence x0, x1, . . . ,xn such that xn = xn−1 + Δx.
  4. Every time a value of xn is generated, we find the value of f(xn).
  5. We look at the signs of f(xn) and f(xn−1).
