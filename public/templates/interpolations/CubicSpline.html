Cubic Spline

Given a function f on [a, b] and nodes a = x0 < x1< · · · < xn = b, a spline (tracer) cubic interpolant p for f is a function that satisfies the following conditions:

  1. p(x) is a cubic polynomial, defined pj(x), over the interval [xj−1, xj] for each j = 1, . . . , n
  2. pj(xj−1) = f(xj−1) and pj(xj ) = f(xj ) for each j = 1, . . . , n
  3. pj+1(xj) = pj(xj) for each j = 1, . . . ,n−1
  4. p′j+1(xj) = p′j(xj) for each j = 1, . . . ,n−1
  5. p′′j+1(xj) = p′′j(xj) for each j = 1, . . . ,n−1
  6. p′′1(x0) = 0 and p′′n(xn) = 0
